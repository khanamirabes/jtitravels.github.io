Package Details

Day 1 - Mussoorie: Arrive Delhi to Reach Mussoorie

Once you reach Delhi airport, meet and greet our tour representative, and get an assisted transfer to the pre-booked hotel at Mussoorie. After reaching, complete your check-in formalities and relax. The rest of the time is free at leisure. Simple unwind at the hotel or take a short stroll on the street of Mussoorie to enjoy delicious street food. Once done, return to the hotel and restore your energy with a sound sleep.

Day 2 - Mussoorie: A Day Reserved for Sightseeing

Wake up early in the morning, enjoy breakfast, and set out for sightseeing in Mussoorie. Enjoy your first visit to Mussoorie Lake, which is an artificially built lake from where you can enjoy amazing views of the Doon valley. Next head towards Gun Hills, the second-highest peak of Mussoorie. Later visit Kempty Falls, which is located on the way between Dehradun-Mussoorie roads. Admire the beautiful location and also take a dip in water falling from an altitude of 40 feet. Next visit Cloud’s end, which is also called the end of Mussoorie. The place is covered by dense jungles, here you can enjoy a lovely nature walk with your loved one. Also, enjoy the amazing views of Aglar River Valley. If you wish you can visit Camel’s Back Road and enjoy a horse ride. Later in the evening take a romantic stroll along the Mall Road and shop for souvenirs. After wonderful sightseeing, get back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3 - Corbett National Park: Enjoy a Memorable Stay

On the 3rd day of your Uttarakhand Honeymoon Tour, wake up early in the morning, get refreshed, and complete the checkout formalities. Today you are going to leave for one of the oldest national parks in India, Jim Corbett National Park. The park is also renowned for covering a wide variety of flora and fauna along with amazing tourism activities. After reaching Corbett National Park, you will be transferred to your pre-booked lodge/wildlife resort. On arrival, complete your check-in formalities and relax. The rest of the day is free at your leisure. Enjoy an overnight stay in a lodge/wildlife resort.

Day 4 - Nainital: Explore National Park and Reach Next Destination

‘Break’ your night-long ‘fast’ at the hotel and get ready to explore Jim Corbett National Park. Go for a jeep safari and observe the wildlife closely. Later, enjoy an elephant ride to explore some more. After enjoying the morning jeep safari and elephant ride, say goodbye to Corbett National Park and head towards Nainital. Best known for its serene beauty and ancient shrines, Nainital is a beautiful hill station located at the green hills of the Kumaon ranges in the Himalayas. On arrival, complete your check-in formalities at the hotel and spend the rest of the day free at leisure. You can unwind at the hotel or can enjoy wandering around Mall Road with your sweetheart in the evening. Once done, get back to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay.

Day 5 - Nainital: Enjoy Sightseeing

Greet the early morning sun and enjoy your hearty breakfast. Later, get set for the wonderful sightseeing in Nainital. This quaint hill station has many things to offer. Visit Naina Peak, which is also called China Peak, is the highest hilltop and one of the major tourist attractions in Nainital. Here you can enjoy trekking and mountaineering with your partner. Next, visit Naina Devi Temple and seek blessings, the temple is located on the shore of Naina Lake. The temple is dedicated to Devi Sati’s eyes. Later, admire the beauty of Naini Lake, which is a natural freshwater lake. Nest, visit the Snow View, one of the famous attractions in Uttarakhand. It offers excellent views of the mighty Himalayas. Later visit the State observatory, a hub of astronomy learning, which is located on the outskirts of Nainital. Later, in the evening take a romantic stroll around the local market of Nainital to enjoy shopping. After sightseeing and exploring the town, get back to the hotel for a sound sleep.

Day 6 - Nainital: Visit Ranikhet and back to Nainital

start your day with a healthy breakfast, and get ready to visit the next beautiful destination in Uttarakhand, Ranikhet. It is a lovely hill station surrounded by dense green forests and barnyards. The cool-pleasant climate and natural beauty of this sumptuous place will calm your senses. On arrival soak in the freshness and get one step closer to nature. Ranikhet is also popular for the striking views of the Nanda Devi Peak. And has amazing hiking ranges, golf courses, ancient temples, and sweet-smelling orchards. After exploring this beautiful place drive back to Nainital to enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7 - Nainital: The Tour Concludes

Here is the refreshing culmination of your Uttarakhand honeymoon tour. Enjoy your delicious breakfast, complete your check-out formalities at the hotel and get an assisted transfer to the Delhi airport for your journey back home.
